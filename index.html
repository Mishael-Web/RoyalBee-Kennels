Services

At RoyalBee Kennels, we mate dogs to produce puppies. We take care of these grown dogs and puppies and we help them find new homes by connecting them to potential owners.

We are adept at assessing root causes of behavior problems with a proven track record of resolving negative behavior patterns, stresses, and social anxieties in dogs, using positive reinforcement and a variety of training methods. We are Knowledgeable about proper nutrition, diet, and exercise for dogs.

We also provide dog security services (K9 Security Services) for your homes and occasions.

About Me

I am the CEO of Royalbee Kennels Nigeria, a leading breeder of high-quality Rottweilers, Caucasian Shepherds, South African Boerboels, and Pug dogs in Lagos Nigeria.

I have been passionate about dogs since I was a child and have been involved in the breeding and care of dogs for over 10 years. I have a degree in Business and I have completed internship and certifications in dog breeding, genetics, and animal care.

My mission at RoyalBee Kennels Nigeria is to provide our clients with healthy, well-bred puppies that are carefully selected for their outstanding temperament, health, and conformation. We take pride in our dogs and are committed to providing them with the best possible care, nutrition, and training.

As the CEO of RoyalBee Kennels Nigeria, I am dedicated to ensuring that our breeding program maintains the highest standards of excellence and that our clients receive exceptional customer service. I am always looking for ways to improve our breeding program and stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in the field of dog breeding.

Thank you for considering RoyalBee Kennels Nigeria for your next furry family member.
